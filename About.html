About Us

The website "FinCorp" was created by Script Slingers on 26th October 2023 for the purpose of providing a one stop solution for the management of personal wealth by reccomending personalisd stocks and stock analysis.



What We Deliver

1) No pre requisite :
Major platforms require the user to have atleast some basic knowledge of financial concepts like budgeting, saving, investing, and debt management, whereas our project eliminate any such requirements for pre requisite knowledge and facilitates the user to build their portfolio from ground up with minimal knowledge about just their income and expenditure.

2) Multiple tools for enhancing portfolio :
-SIP calculator : SIP calculator is a simple tool that allows individuals to get an idea of the returns on their mutual fund investments made through SIP.
-Mutual Fund Recommendation : It provides personalized recommendation according to user goals inculcating the following ways:
Long-Term Goals: Choose Equity, Large-Cap, or Index Funds for high, sustained growth.
Short-Term Goals: Opt for Debt, Liquid, or Ultra Short-Term Funds for stability and liquidity.
Consider SIPs: Systematic Investment Plans offer disciplined investing for both goals.
3) Broad Insight about leading stocks :
Comprehensive Analysis: Our website offers in-depth analyses of leading stocks, covering key financial metrics, historical performance, and market trends.
News and Updates: Stay updated with real-time news and developments affecting major stocks.
Comparative Data: Users can compare leading stocks side by side, examining their performance.
Historical Data: Access historical data of leading stocks, enabling users to conduct thorough backtesting and research, aiding in long-term investment strategies.
